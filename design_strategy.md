# Portfolio Overhaul Strategy: "The High-Impact Engineer"

## 1. Visual Identity
- **Theme**: Sophisticated Dark Mode (FinTech/AI aesthetic).
- **Color Palette**:
  - Background: `#0D1117` (Deep Charcoal)
  - Surface: `#161B22` (Dark Gray)
  - Primary Accent: `#58A6FF` (Electric Blue)
  - Secondary Accent: `#7EE787` (Emerald Green - for "results" and "growth")
  - Text: `#C9D1D9` (Soft White/Gray)
- **Typography**:
  - Headings: `Inter` (Bold, tight tracking)
  - Body: `Inter` (Regular, high line-height)
  - Technical: `JetBrains Mono` (For tech stacks and metrics)
- **Layout**: Bento-style grid for projects and skills to maximize "information density" without clutter.

## 2. Result-Oriented Copywriting Strategy
- **SBA Lead Sourcing**: "Engineered a scalable data pipeline for 1.87M records; implemented semantic search (HNSW) and multi-fallback contact enrichment to drive lead generation efficiency."
- **Ingram Micro**: "Architected deterministic AI agents for Microsoft Copilot, automating high-friction ETL workflows and reducing operational overhead by [estimated]%."
- **SUNY Polytechnic**: "Streamlined academic evaluations via Selenium automation, delivering a 33% reduction in processing time."

## 3. Structure
- **Hero**: Bold statement: "Building Deterministic AI & Scalable Data Systems."
- **Metrics Bar**: Quick stats (1.8M+ Records, 33% Efficiency Gain, 9B-12B Model Optimization).
- **Project Bento**: High-impact cards for SBA and LM Studio projects.
- **Experience**: Timeline with bullet points starting with Action Verbs (Engineered, Architected, Optimized).
- **Contact**: Minimalist, high-conversion footer.
